About This Information Center

General

With changes to travel regulations during the COVID-19 pandemic, travellers must be well-informed when they make travel decisions. The data-gathering process can be overwhelming due to different countries imposing different regulations. This is where this centralized information center comes in to help! This is an interactive information center that gives you all the information you need about your destination including (but not limited to) travel restrictions, quarantine information, and local policies. Just input your country of departure & your vaccination status and choose a destination country and we will display all travel information applicable to you, filtering out any unrelated policies.

How to Use

User Information

Input your information regarding your country of departure and your vaccination status for a personalized information.

The Map

Hover around and select the country of destination. If you cannot find them, just search by the country name! If you are unsure where to travel to, use our filter to highlight only countries that matches with your requirements.

The Data

Once you have selected a country of destination, COVID-19 graphs & data of that country will be generated underneath the map alongside local & travel policies that are specific to travellers from your country of departure and with similar vaccination status as yours.
